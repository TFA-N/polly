Script Description
Scoreboard Paster Posts Revorix scoreboard to polly
Battlereport Paster Posts Reovix battle reports to polly.
Auto Remind HOWTO: edit the installed script and insert your polly login data (this is important!), then edit your user settings to enable auto reminds. You may also configure different AZ values for different fleets here.
Auto Reminds only work if you are currently known to polly in IRC (i.e. you currently share at least one channel and you are logged in).

This script is also capable of showing a warning if one ship drops below a certain pz value. This can also be configured on your user page.
